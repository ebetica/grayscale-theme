The drive from NYC to New Paltz is the easiest, but not only, method of getting there. Depending on where and when you leave the city it takes a little less than 2 hours. Want to carpool? Driving and willing to bring passengers? Please let us know!

There are busses which travel between New York Penn Station and New Paltz throughout the day. Round trip tickets cost $46. The bus will take you to downtown New Paltz, where a short taxi or lyft ride will get you to our location. We recommend leaving Penn Station on the 1 PM bus, which gets you into New Paltz at 2:30, in plenty of time to get a ride to the ceremony. The last return bus of the day leaves at 9pm. Poughkeepsie station is about 25 minutes drive from our wedding location. Metro North runs trains from Grand Central to Poughkeepsie which take 2 hours, and cost about $40 round trip. We recommend taking the 1:45 train arriving in Poughkeepsie at 3:45 and getting a Lyft or taxi to our location. Return trains depart Poughkeepsie at 8:45, 9:50 and 11:48 PM.
