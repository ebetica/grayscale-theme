Getting There

🚗Driving🚗

The drive from NYC to New Paltz is the easiest, but not only, method of getting there. Depending on where and when you leave the city it takes a little less than 2 hours. Want to carpool? Driving and willing to bring passengers? Please let us know!

🚌Bus🚌

There are busses which travel between New York Penn Station and New Paltz throughout the day. Round trip tickets cost $46. The bus will take you to downtown New Paltz, where a short taxi or lyft ride will get you to our location. We recommend leaving Penn Station on the 1 PM bus, which gets you into New Paltz at 2:30, in plenty of time to get a ride to the ceremony. The last return bus of the day leaves at 9pm. The full schedule is here

🚆Train🚆

Poughkeepsie station is about 25 minutes drive from our wedding location. Metro North runs trains from Grand Central to Poughkeepsie which take 2 hours, and cost about $40 round trip. We recommend taking the 1:45 train arriving in Poughkeepsie at 3:45 and getting a Lyft or taxi to our location. Return trains depart Poughkeepsie at 8:45, 9:50 and 11:48 PM. You can see the full schedule at Metro-North - Schedules, Tickets, rail


Where to Stay

🏕️🏕️🏕️

These are suggestions - we are not associated with any of the places listed, and this is not an exhaustive list at all. But keep in mind October is peak ‘leaf-peeping’ season, so accommodation may book up quickly, and definitely make reservations in advance.

Minnewaska Lodge: 20 minutes drive from our location, this lodge is set in nature near Lake Minnewaska State park and the Mohonk Preserve.
Hampton Inn New Paltz: 10 minutes drive from the wedding location.
Best Western Kingston: 20 minutes drive from the wedding location in Kingston, the next small town North of New Paltz. We’ve stayed there before - it’s fine.
The Gunks American Alpine Association Campground: 15 minutes drive from the wedding location. For all you rock climbing dirtbags out there ;)
Airbnb and VRBO: both have plenty of accommodations in the area. Good for groups!
